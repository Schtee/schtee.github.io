I have created a new project! It works alongside [Tiled2Unity](http://www.seanba.com/tiled2unity) to ease importing [Tiled](http://www.mapeditor.org/) maps into Unity. It is:
[Tiled2UnityAutoImport](https://github.com/Schtee/Tiled2UnityAutoImport).  

In a nutshell, it hooks into Unity's asset importing flow and automatically runs Tiled2Unity when new/modified tmx maps are found. More details can be found in the project's [README](https://github.com/Schtee/Tiled2UnityAutoImport/blob/master/README.md).
